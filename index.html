About

Studio Calix is a portfolio of Fransiska Calista, a young interior designer from Semarang, Indonesia. Specializes in designing spaces with modern-contemporary style and simplistic color palette, as Studio Calix optimizes the function of one space according to its user's needs while maintaining the aesthetic.

Projects

Studio Apartment

A studio apartment basically is a unit where everything except the bathroom area uses single room, wall-less space. Studio apartments usually has up to 25 square meters of space. The chosen style for this design is Modern-Meditteranean, which main palette is natural and earthy colors.

Open Kitchen

An open-concept kitchen refers to a lack of barriers in the kitchen and surrounding areas. Typically, this includes the dining room and living room. However this kitchen is placed at the back of the main building of the house, thus instead of combined with the main area, the kitchen is combined with the backyard.

Loft Apartment

A loft can be an upper story or attic in a building, directly under the roof. A loft apartment refers to large adaptable open space, often converted for residential use. Loft apartments are known for their high ceilings and spacious rooms, with bedroom and living area separated by room level.

Office Space

An office is generally a building, room or other area where an organization's employees perform administrative work in order to support and realize objects and goals of the organization. In designing office spaces, the important notes is not only the aesthetic look because the choice of furnitures, technologies, and materials are also to keep in mind.
